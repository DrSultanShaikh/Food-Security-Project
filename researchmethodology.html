Research Methodology

Advanced approaches for sustainable aquaculture development in Qatar

Implementation Strategy

The project will be implemented through a phased approach, ensuring systematic progression from laboratory-scale optimization to pilot-scale validation over 36 months.

Lab Scale Months 1-12
Pilot Scale Months 13-24
Trials & Analysis Months 25-36
01

Phase 1: Laboratory Scale

Months 1-12

Small-scale reactor optimization, parameter screening, and preliminary digestibility studies.

Key Activities:

  • Reactor design and optimization
  • Parameter screening (light, temperature, nutrients)
  • Preliminary digestibility studies
  • Biomass quality assessment
  • Initial feed formulation development

Expected Outputs:

Optimized reactor parameters Initial biomass characterization Digestibility baseline data Preliminary feed formulations
02

Phase 2: Pilot Scale

Months 13-24

Scale-up to pilot reactors, continuous process optimization, and comprehensive feed formulation.

Key Activities:

  • Pilot reactor installation and operation
  • Continuous process optimization
  • Advanced feed formulation development
  • Quality control protocols establishment
  • Preparation for aquaculture trials

Expected Outputs:

Pilot-scale production data Optimized feed formulations Quality control framework Trial preparation protocols
03

Phase 3: Trials & Analysis

Months 25-36

Aquaculture feeding trials, comprehensive data analysis, sustainability assessment, and final reporting.

Key Activities:

  • Controlled feeding trials with target species
  • Growth performance monitoring and analysis
  • Health and nutritional assessment
  • Life cycle assessment and techno-economic analysis
  • Comprehensive data analysis and final reporting

Expected Outputs:

Growth performance data Health assessment reports Sustainability analysis Final research report

Project Duration

36 Months Total
